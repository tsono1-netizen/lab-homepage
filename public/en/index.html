# Sono Laboratory

**AI × Spine Surgery × Translational Research**

Sono Laboratory integrates spine surgery, orthopedic imaging, artificial intelligence, and molecular biology to develop next-generation diagnostic and therapeutic strategies.

## Research Topics

- AI-assisted spinal imaging

- Spine surgery and sagittal alignment

- OPLL and ligament ossification research

- LLIF / OLIF biomechanics

- Digital orthopedics

- Scaffold-free artificial spinal cord

## Mission

We aim to translate clinically driven questions into innovative solutions through imaging analysis, AI, basic science, and medical-engineering collaboration.
